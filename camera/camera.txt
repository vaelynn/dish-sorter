heres the code i used for the camera. 

i relied heavily on indrekkluk's youtube video, using his arduino code and his arduimagecapture plugin for testing. 

i used loveable to create the website.
https://id-preview--d1acd5a2-8328-42bd-b843-045cfea69a57.lovable.app/
due to the ai having a high bias towards forks, i made the threshold for a spoon 48% and for a fork, 80%.
they must remain at their threshold (or above) for 4 seconds before the signal is sent to the servo to carry out movement.

green screen for a few seconds when you connect to the arduino means the camera is initiating.
